  • Mirror sphere

    Reuben's still at a very early stage of the design process. He to build a full size model of a facade unit in order to prove the concept. The design is difficult to explain briefly, but in essence he want to create a weatherproof surface composed of multiple spherical ETFE pillows. He has a design for a double axis heliostat mechanism which he wants to enclose within some of the pillows. I've attached some images. He's used a regular spherical surface in the example but he wants to be able to apply this to irregular surfaces too. Notice the concentric red then blue rings housing a circular surface. These represent the heliostats which he wants to enclose with ETFE. He also need to seal the spaces between the spheres of ETFE.
